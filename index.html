About Me

I find ways to relate what I already know, to to what I don't understand. I speak English, Spanish, and German. Prior to Software Engineering, I was a Quality Manager in manufacturing for four years. I worked in Manufacturing for Eight years. I most enjoyed seeing products grow from Concept/Kickstarter to mass-manufacture in full production. It was highly engaging to speak at any level to all technical levels to the cross-functional teams I was exposed to. I enjoyed being the go-to Subject Matter Expert. I personally think full-immersion is the best way to learn a subject matter. As a software developer, I have honed in on utilizing React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, HTML and CSS to broaden my web development skills and technical abillities. Whether it is 3d-printing, woodworking, or developing a new application, I have always enjoyed exploring new technologies and gaining a deeper level of understanding of what new possibilities exist with that technology. Problems are troubleshooting oppourtunities to develop a creative solution. When bringing an idea to life, the ease of use and user experience is important to me, so I strive to design an interface that is intuitive, innovative, appealing for the user.
