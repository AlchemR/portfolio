About Me

I think full-immersion is the most effective way to learn any subject matter. I speak English, Spanish, and German. Prior to Software, I was a Quality Manager / Quality Engineer for 8 years in manufacturing. My most gratifying professional acheivements involved facilitating products growth from Concept/Kickstarter to full production. I enjoyed being the go-to Subject Matter Expert of the entire manufacturing Process. It was rewarding to speak to any technical level of the cross-functional teams I was exposed to. As a software developer, I have honed in on utilizing React, Redux, Ruby on Rails, Javascript, HTML and CSS to broaden my web development skillset and technical abillities. Whether it is Software, 3d-printing or woodworking, I have always enjoyed exploring new technologies and gaining a deeper level of understanding of what new possibilities exist with that technology. Problems are troubleshooting oppourtunities to develop creative solutions. When bringing an idea to life, the ease of use and user experience is important to me, so I strive to design an interface that is intuitive, innovative, appealing for the user.
